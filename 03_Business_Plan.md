# Business Plan: SuryaClean — Solar Panel Cleaning Services

## Executive Summary

**SuryaClean** is a tech-enabled solar panel cleaning and maintenance company launching in Faridabad, Haryana, with expansion plans across Delhi NCR. We solve the problem of efficiency loss due to dirty solar panels — which can reduce output by **15-30%** — through professional, water-efficient cleaning and an app-based booking platform. Our target market includes residential homeowners, housing societies, commercial buildings, and industrial solar installations in the rapidly growing Faridabad-Gurgaon-Noida solar corridor.

**Mission**: To maximize India's solar investment returns by making professional panel maintenance as easy as booking a cab.

**Vision**: To become the #1 solar O&M (Operations & Maintenance) service provider in North India within 5 years, managing 1 GW+ of solar installations.

## 3. Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors (Faridabad Area)
| Competitor | Strength | Weakness | Our Advantage |
|-----------|----------|----------|---------------|
| Local unorganized cleaners (mali, domestic help) | Cheap (₹200-₹500) | No training, damage risk, inconsistent | Professional, insured, guaranteed |
| Solar installers offering AMC | Existing relationship | Cleaning not core focus, expensive (₹8-15K/yr) | Specialized, better price |
| Facility management companies | Large contracts | High minimum, no residential focus | Flexible, any size job |
| DIY (homeowner self-clean) | Free | Risky (roof fall, scratches), inconsistent, no diagnostics | Safety, quality, diagnostics included |

### Competitive Moat
1. **Tech Layer**: App-based booking, real-time tracking, digital payment — no competitor offers this in Faridabad
2. **Quality Assurance**: Mandatory before/after photos, GPS-verified visits, post-service rating
3. **Water-Efficient Tech**: Environmentally responsible, crucial for water-scarce Faridabad
4. **AMC Lock-in**: Recurring revenue creates switching costs

## 5. Risk Analysis & Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Low demand / low awareness | Medium | High | Free demos, educational content (blog/videos), partner with installers to recommend cleaning at time of installation |
| Worker safety (roof falls) | Medium | Critical | Mandatory safety harness, safety training certification, insurance for all workers (₹10L coverage each) |
| Panel damage liability | Low | Critical | Comprehensive insurance, pre-inspection photo documentation, trained staff only |
| Seasonal demand (monsoon dip) | High | Medium | Push AMC (steady income), offer monsoon specials (discount to fill lean period), expand to indoor cleaning services |
| Price undercutting by locals | High | Medium | Compete on quality/trust not price, emphasize ROI (cleaning pays for itself via higher output), AMC locks in customers |
| Water scarcity in Faridabad | Medium | Medium | Invest in water-efficient/waterless cleaning tech, rainwater harvesting for own operations, position as eco-friendly |
| Worker attrition | Medium | High | Above-market pay, performance bonuses, upskilling (thermal camera training), clear career path |
| App development delays/cost overruns | Medium | Low | Start with website + WhatsApp booking (no-code launch), build app once revenue flowing |
