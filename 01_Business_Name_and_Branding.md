# Business Name & Branding Strategy

## Recommended Business Name: **SuryaClean**

### Why SuryaClean?
- **Surya** (सूर्य): The Sun god in Hindu mythology — directly tied to solar energy. Instantly recognizable and culturally resonant across North India, especially in Haryana/UP/NCR.
- **Clean**: Clear, functional, says exactly what you do. No ambiguity.
- Together: "Sun Clean" — short, memorable, bilingual-friendly, easy to pronounce in both Hindi and English.
- Domain availability is high for variations (see domain strategy doc).

### Alternative Names (Backup Options)
| Name | Rationale | Best For |
|------|-----------|----------|
| **HelioWash** | Helios (Greek sun god) + Wash — sounds premium/tech-forward | Premium housing societies |
| **SolarSafai** | Bilingual (Solar + Safai = cleanliness in Hindi) | Mass market, vernacular appeal |
| **PanelPro** | Professional, suggests expertise | Commercial/industrial clients |
| **SunShine Clean** | Positive, evokes brightness after cleaning | Residential homeowners |
| **DhoopCare** | Dhoop (धूप = sunlight) + Care — warm, caring brand | Long-term maintenance contracts |

### Brand Identity Guidelines

#### Tagline Options
- **Primary**: "Har Panel, Har Din, Chamakdaar" (Every Panel, Every Day, Shining Bright)
- **English**: "Power Through Purity"
- **Short**: "Clean Panels. More Power."

#### Brand Colors
| Color | Hex Code | Usage |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Solar Gold/Amber | `#F5A623` | Logo, primary CTA buttons |
| Sky Blue | `#4A90D9` | Trust elements, app header |
| Clean White | `#FFFFFF` | Backgrounds, cards |
| Deep Navy | `#1B2A4A` | Text, footer, premium elements |
| Leaf Green | `#27AE60` | Eco-friendly badges, sustainability messaging |

#### Logo Concept
- A stylized sun with water droplet(s) integrated into the rays
- A solar panel grid pattern subtly embedded in the sun circle
- Clean sans-serif font (Poppins or Montserrat) for "SuryaClean"
- Optional: A small roof-line silhouette below for B2C variant

#### Brand Voice
- **Tone**: Professional yet approachable, Hindi-English mix (Hinglish) for local audience
- **Values**: Trustworthy, Eco-conscious, Tech-savvy, Affordable
- **Key Messaging Pillars**:
  1. Increase your solar ROI by 15-30% (data-backed)
  2. Water-efficient cleaning technology (eco-angle)
  3. Trained, verified, insured professionals
  4. Book in 60 seconds via app

### Trademark & Registration Checklist
- [ ] Trademark search on IP India portal for "SuryaClean" (Classes 37 & 40 — cleaning/maintenance services)
- [ ] Register as a Sole Proprietorship / LLP / Pvt Ltd (recommend LLP initially for liability protection)
- [ ] GST registration (required for service business above ₹20L turnover in Haryana)
- [ ] Udyam (MSME) registration for government scheme benefits
- [ ] Shop & Establishment Act license (Faridabad Municipal Corporation)
- [ ] Trademark application for logo + name (₹4,500-₹9,000 govt fee + lawyer costs)

### Sub-Brand / Product Line Naming
- **SuryaClean One-Time**: Ad-hoc cleaning service
- **SuryaClean AMC**: Annual Maintenance Contract (quarterly/monthly)
- **SuryaClean Pro**: Commercial/industrial large-scale cleaning
- **SuryaClean Green**: Eco-friendly waterless cleaning variant
- **SuryaSmart**: IoT monitoring add-on (future upsell)

### Mascot Idea
- **"Surya Bhai"** — A friendly animated sun character for app UI and marketing materials
- Wears a worker's cap and carries a squeegee
- Appears in onboarding, notifications, and social media
- Appeals to both kids (family homes) and adults (relatable)

---

*This naming and branding strategy is designed to work across Hindi and English markets, be memorable for word-of-mouth referrals, and convey professionalism and trust from day one.*
