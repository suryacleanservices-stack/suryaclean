## 11. Logistics & Supply Chain Optimization

### Equipment Distribution to Teams
- Each team starts the day from the central office
- Equipment checked out via Google Sheets (or app) at 7:30 AM
- Each bike carries: 1 water-fed pole, 1 pump + battery, 1 brush set, 1 squeegee set, microfiber, chemicals (in small bottles), 1 harness, 1 helmet, 1 first aid kit
- Water: Teams use **customer's water supply** (tap on roof/terrace). In rare cases where no water access is available, they carry 100L portable tank filled from office
- End-of-day equipment check-in at office by 5 PM
- Supervisor inspects all equipment for damage/wear

### Water Sourcing Strategy
- **Primary**: Customer's water supply (terrace tap) — 90% of jobs. Free. Included in service price.
- **Secondary**: Portable 100L tank filled at office — for homes without terrace access. Cost: negligible (municipal water).
- **Emergency**: Private water tanker (₹500-₹800 per 5,000L) if office supply is disrupted. Only in extreme cases.

### Chemical Mixing & Distribution
- Mix cleaning solution concentrate in 50L drum at office (dilute per manufacturer ratio)
- Fill 5L portable containers for each team daily
- Each 5L container covers 6-8 residential jobs
- Teams carry 2×5L containers per day (10L total = 12-16 jobs capacity)
