## 3. Worker Pay Model

### Option A: Fixed Salary + Per-Job Bonus (Recommended)
This model incentives productivity while providing income stability.

| Component | Amount | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| **Base Salary** | ₹8,000 - ₹10,000/month | Guaranteed, even if no jobs (covers lean periods) |
| **Per-Job Bonus** | ₹50 - ₹100 per completed job | Incentivizes efficiency. Avg worker does 4-6 jobs/day |
| **Travel Allowance** | ₹1,500 - ₹2,000/month | For bike fuel or bus fare |
| **Attendance Bonus** | ₹500/month | For zero unexcused absences in a month |
| **5-Star Bonus** | ₹50 per 5-star review | Quality incentive |

**Expected Monthly Earnings (Avg Worker)**:
- Base salary: ₹9,000
- Per-job bonus (22 working days × 5 jobs × ₹75 avg): ₹8,250
- Travel allowance: ₹1,500
- Attendance bonus: ₹500
- 5-star bonus (15 reviews): ₹750
- **Total: ~₹20,000/month**

### Option B: Pure Gig / Per-Job Model
- **Per residential job**: ₹150 - ₹250 (depending on panel count)
- **Per commercial job**: ₹300 - ₹500
- No base salary, no benefits
- Better for seasonal / part-time workers
- **Use this for overflow/demand spikes**, not core team

### Option C: Daily Wage + Performance Incentive
- **Daily wage**: ₹400 - ₹500/day
- **Minimum jobs required**: 4/day
- **Overtime**: ₹100 per extra job beyond 4
- **Suitable for**: Initial pilot phase or short-term hires

### Payment Schedule
- Salary disbursed by 5th of every month
- Weekly advance of ₹1,000 available for workers
- Per-job bonus computed and paid with salary
- All payments via bank transfer (mandatory bank account) — builds financial inclusion

## 4. Hiring Strategy & Channels

### Where to Find Workers

| Channel | Best For | Cost |
|---------|----------|------|
| **Local Labour Chowk (Faridabad)** | Entry-level cleaning workers | Free (time cost) |
| **OLX / Quikr Jobs** | Semi-skilled workers, team leaders | ₹500-2,000 per listing |
| **WhatsApp Groups** | Referrals from existing workers | Free (₹500 referral bonus) |
| **Apna.co / WorkIndia App** | Blue-collar hiring platform — best for this segment | Free to low cost |
| **Local Newspaper (Dainik Jagran Faridabad)** | Generic hiring | ₹500-1,000 per ad |
| **ITI / Vocational Training Centers** | Slightly more skilled (electrician background) | Free |
| **Staffing Agency (e.g., TeamLease, Quess)** | Bulk hiring for expansion | 8-12% of annual salary |
| **NGOs / Skill India Centers** | Government-supported hiring, CSR angle | Free |

### Hiring SOP
1. **Post job listing** on Apna.co + OLX + local WhatsApp groups
2. **Phone screen**: Confirm age, location (max 10km from Faridabad office), smartphone ownership, comfort with heights
3. **In-person interview**: Check physical fitness, verify ID (Aadhaar), check for criminal record (basic)
4. **Practical test**: Climb a mock ladder, demonstrate basic tool handling
5. **Document collection**: Aadhaar card, bank passbook, 2 passport photos, police verification form
6. **3-day paid trial**: Shadow experienced worker, learn safety + cleaning process
7. **Onboarding**: Uniform, ID card, app account created, added to WhatsApp group

## 5. Training Program

### Initial Training (5 Days)

| Day | Module | Duration | Topics |
|-----|--------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Safety First | 4 hours | Roof safety, harness usage, electrical safety (panels are live!), first aid, heat stroke prevention |
| 2 | Panel Basics | 4 hours | Types of panels (mono/poly), how solar works, what damages panels, what to avoid |
| 3 | Cleaning Technique | 6 hours | Water-fed pole method, squeegee technique, cleaning solution mixing, stubborn stain removal, water pressure limits |
| 4 | Tool & Equipment | 4 hours | Operating water pump, telescopic pole, brush heads, water tank management, equipment maintenance |
| 5 | App & Customer Service | 4 hours | Using SuryaClean worker app, check-in/out, photo upload, customer interaction, upselling script |
| + | Practical On-Job | 3 days | Shadow experienced worker on real jobs |

### Ongoing Training (Monthly)
- **1st Saturday**: Safety refresher (30 mins)
- **Last Saturday**: New technique/skill (thermal camera use, bird mesh installation, panel inspection)
- **Quarterly**: Expert guest session (solar installer shares insights)

### Safety Certification
- Partner with **National Safety Council of India** or **Disaster Management Institute** for certified safety training
- All workers must pass safety test before being assigned solo jobs
- Annual re-certification

## 7. Worker-to-Job Ratio & Scheduling

### Capacity Planning
- **1 worker** can complete **4-6 residential jobs/day** (avg 45 min per job + travel)
- **1 team** (2 workers) can handle **8-10 residential jobs/day**
- **1 commercial job** takes 3-6 hours for a 2-person team
- **Maximum jobs/worker/day**: 8 (small residential, clustered geographically)
- **Optimal route density**: 5-7 jobs within a 5km radius

### Scheduling Logic
- Jobs auto-assigned by app based on:
  1. Worker proximity to job location (Google Maps API)
  2. Worker availability (not on leave, not overloaded)
  3. Skill match (commercial jobs go to experienced workers)
- Buffer time: 15 min between jobs for travel
- Morning shift: 7 AM - 1 PM (summer) or 8 AM - 2 PM (winter)
- Evening shift (optional): 3 PM - 6 PM (for working professionals who need weekend slots)

## 8. Worker Compliance & Legal

### Labour Law Compliance (Haryana)
- **Contract Labour Act (Regulation & Abolition) Act, 1970**: Applicable if 20+ contract workers
- **Minimum Wages Act**: Haryana minimum wage for unskilled workers: ~₹10,000-₹11,000/month (2024-25). Our model pays above this.
- **Building & Other Construction Workers (BOCW) Act**: May apply since roof work is "construction adjacent." Register with Haryana BOCW Welfare Board. Provides accidental death cover, pension, education assistance for workers' children. Cess: 1% of project cost (but for service businesses, this is typically exempt — consult labour lawyer).
- **Inter-State Migrant Workmen Act**: If hiring workers from UP/Bihar/Rajasthan, registration required.
- **Sexual Harassment (POSH)**: Internal Complaints Committee required if 10+ employees. Zero-tolerance policy.
