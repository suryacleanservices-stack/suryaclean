### International Suppliers (For Premium Equipment)
| Supplier | Product | Import Viability |
|----------|---------|-----------------|
| **IPC Eagle** (USA) | High-end water-fed poles & pure water systems | Expensive shipping + customs (40-60%), not recommended initially |
| **Unger Germany** | Premium squeegees, microfiber | Available via Indian distributors |
| **Alibaba / Aliexpress** | Budget water-fed poles, pumps | Risk of quality, warranty issues. Avoid for core equipment. |

### Sourcing Tips
- **Water-fed poles**: Buy from Amazon India or industrial cleaning suppliers in Delhi. Test quality before bulk order.
- **Brushes**: Source from Sadar Bazar Delhi — significantly cheaper than branded options. Buy 5-10 units at once for discount.
- **Safety harness**: DO NOT compromise. Buy ISI-marked only. Karam Industries (Noida) is a reliable Indian manufacturer.
- **Chemicals**: Chemtex (Kolkata) or Ecolab (Pune) for bulk. Start with smaller quantities (10-20L) and test on scrap panels.
- **Bikes**: Buy used bikes (2-3 years old) to reduce CapEx. OLX/Quikr Faridabad. Ensure RC transfer + insurance.

### Inventory Tracking
- Manual tracking via **Google Sheets** for first 6 months (free, simple)
- Upgrade to **Zoho Inventory** (₹2,499/month) when 3+ teams
- Barcode/QR code on equipment for check-in/check-out tracking
- Equipment issued to workers — signed responsibility form
- Monthly physical stock audit by supervisor

### Equipment Maintenance Schedule

| Equipment | Maintenance | Frequency |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| Water Pump | Check pressure output, clean filter, test battery charge | Weekly |
| Water-fed Pole | Check clamps, clean brush, lubricate extension joints | Weekly |
