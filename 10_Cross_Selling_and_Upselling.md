# Cross-Selling & Upselling Strategy

## 1. The Upsell Funnel Philosophy

> **"You've already won the hardest battle — getting inside the customer's home/rooftop. Now maximize the lifetime value of that relationship."**

The solar panel cleaning visit is a **trust-building touchpoint**. Your worker spends 45 minutes on the customer's roof. They see the entire solar ecosystem — panels, inverter, wiring, mounting structure, bird activity, water pooling, shading issues. This is your opportunity to identify and offer additional solutions.

**Golden Rule**: Recommend, don't hard-sell. Position as "We noticed this during your cleaning — would you like us to take care of it?"

## 4. The Upsell Conversation Framework

### "P.R.O." Method: **Problem → Recommendation → Outcome**

#### Example 1: Upselling AMC After One-Time Clean

**Problem** (Show evidence):
> "Sir, aaj cleaning ke baad aapke panels bilkul saaf ho gaye hain. Lekin maine notice kiya ki aapki location par construction kaafi ho raha hai — iska matlab 3-4 hafte mein phir se dhool jamegi. Aap dekh sakte hain before photo mein kitni dhool thi sirf 2 mahine mein."

*(Sir, after cleaning your panels are perfectly clean. But I noticed there's a lot of construction near your location — this means dust will accumulate again in 3-4 weeks. You can see in the before photo how much dust built up in just 2 months.)*

**Recommendation** (Present solution):
> "Isliye main suggest karunga SuryaClean Standard AMC — ₹2,999/year, 4 baar cleaning. Har 3 mahine mein hum aake saaf kar denge. ₹250 mahina — ek chai-samosay se bhi kam."

*(That's why I'd suggest the SuryaClean Standard AMC — ₹2,999/year, 4 cleanings. Every 3 months we'll come and clean. ₹250 per month — less than a cup of tea and snack.)*

**Outcome** (Quantify benefit):
> "Regular cleaning se aapki generation 20% zyada rahegi. 5 kW system par iska matlab ₹10,000-₹12,000 ki bachat har saal. AMC ka kharcha ₹2,999 — net bachat ₹7,000-₹9,000."

*(With regular cleaning, your generation will stay 20% higher. On a 5 kW system, that means ₹10,000-₹12,000 savings per year. AMC costs ₹2,999 — net saving ₹7,000-₹9,000.)*

**Close**:
> "Kya main aapka enrollment abhi kar doon? Pehli AMC visit ka appointment aaj hi book ho jayega."

*(Should I enroll you right now? Your first AMC visit will be booked immediately.)*

---

#### Example 2: Upselling Bird Mesh

**Problem**:
> "Sir, cleaning ke time maine dekha ki aapke panels ke neeche kabootar (pigeons) ne ghosla bana rakha hai. Unki droppings se panel surface damage ho sakta hai — acidic hoti hai, coating kharab kar deti hai. Aur neeche wiring bhi kharab ho sakti hai."

*(Sir, during cleaning I saw pigeons have made nests under your panels. Their droppings are acidic and can damage the panel surface coating. The wiring underneath can also get damaged.)*

**Recommendation**:
> "Hum bird mesh installation karte hain — panels ke chaaron taraf jaali laga dete hain. Phir kabootar andar nahi ghus sakte. Aapki chhat bhi saaf rahegi, panels bhi safe."

*(We do bird mesh installation — we put netting around the panels so pigeons can't get under them. Your roof stays clean, panels stay safe.)*

**Outcome**:
> "Ek baar lagwane ke baad 3-5 saal tak koi problem nahi. Cleaning mein bhi aasan hoti hai — bird droppings nahi honge toh extra chemical charge nahi lagega."

*(Once installed, no problems for 3-5 years. Cleaning also becomes easier — without bird droppings, you won't need extra chemical charges.)*

**Price & Close**:
> "Aapke system ke size ke hisaab se ₹4,500-₹5,500 ke beech mein aaega. Main agle Saturday subah team bhej sakta hoon installation ke liye. Kya time suit karega?"

*(For your system size, it'll be between ₹4,500-₹5,500. I can send a team next Saturday morning for installation. Does that time work?)*

## 5. Timing: When to Pitch Each Upsell

| Touchpoint | Best Upsell to Pitch | Why |
|------------|---------------------|-----|
| **During/Right After Cleaning** | AMC Upgrade, Thermal Imaging, Deep Stain Treatment | Customer sees quality of work; trust is highest |
| **Before/After Photo Review** | Anti-Soiling Coating (if showing heavy buildup) | Visual proof of problem creates urgency |
| **When Worker Spots Bird Activity** | Bird Mesh Installation | Problem is visible and current |
| **When Customer Complains About Generation** | Thermal Imaging, Inverter Check, Monitoring System | Customer is already aware of a problem — solution sell |
| **End of AMC Term (Renewal Call)** | Upgrade to Premium AMC, Add Thermal Imaging | Customer is already committed; moving up is easier than new sale |
| **Diwali/New Year** | Gift a DIY kit, promote products | Festival = spending mindset |
| **Referral Thank-You** | Offer Premium AMC upgrade as referral reward | Rewarding loyalty builds lifetime value |
| **Complaint Resolution** | Offer free upgrade to Premium for 1 quarter | Turning a negative into a positive |

## 6. Worker Training for Upselling

### What Workers Must Know
1. **How to identify upsell opportunities**: Checklist on worker app
   - [ ] Bird droppings or nests? → Bird mesh
   - [ ] Heavy dust despite recent rain? → Anti-soiling coating
   - [ ] Inverter making noise/looking old? → Inverter check
   - [ ] Water pooling near panels? → Gutter cleaning
   - [ ] Panels in shade part of day? → Monitoring/reconfiguration
   - [ ] Customer asking "generation kum kyun hai"? → Thermal imaging

2. **How to pitch (script in Hindi)**:
   - Start with "Maine aapke panels ki safai karte waqt notice kiya..." (I noticed while cleaning your panels...)
   - Focus on problem + cost of NOT fixing it
   - Never pressure — "Aap soch lijiye, main agle visit mein puch lunga" (Think about it, I'll ask on the next visit)

3. **What NOT to do**:
   - Don't scare the customer ("Aapke panels kharab ho jayenge!")
   - Don't create false problems to upsell
   - Don't push if customer says no twice
   - Don't quote price without checking with office (for complex jobs)

### Worker Incentive Structure
| Upsell Achieved | Worker Commission |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| One-time → AMC conversion | ₹200-₹300 |
| Thermal imaging add-on | ₹100 |
| Bird mesh lead (converted) | ₹300-₹500 |
| Anti-soiling coating | ₹100 |
| DIY kit sale | ₹50 |
| Any product sale | 10% of margin |
