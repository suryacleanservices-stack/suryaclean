## 2. Channel-by-Channel Execution Plan

### Channel 1: Google Business Profile (FREE — Highest ROI)

**Why It's #1**: When someone searches "solar panel cleaning near me" or "solar cleaning Faridabad," Google Business Profile results appear FIRST — above organic results. This is 60%+ of your local leads.

**Setup Checklist**:
- [ ] Create profile at google.com/business
- [ ] Business name: "SuryaClean — Solar Panel Cleaning & AMC"
- [ ] Category: "Solar Energy Company" + "Cleaning Service"
- [ ] Address: Your office address in Faridabad (even if home-based initially, use a service area business setting)
- [ ] Service areas: Faridabad, Ballabgarh, Palwal, Gurgaon, Noida, South Delhi
- [ ] Phone number (call-capable, not just WhatsApp)
- [ ] Website link: suryaclean.in
- [ ] Business hours: Mon-Sat 8 AM - 6 PM, Sun 9 AM - 2 PM
- [ ] Photos: Upload 20+ high-quality photos — before/after cleaning, workers in uniform, equipment, office
- [ ] Videos: 30-sec time-lapse of a panel clean
- [ ] Products/Services: List each service with price range
- [ ] Q&A: Pre-populate 10 FAQs with answers

**Ongoing Optimization**:
- **Post weekly**: Photo of a completed job, customer testimonial, cleaning tip
- **Respond to ALL reviews** within 24 hours (positive: thank + mention service; negative: apologize + offer resolution)
- **Review generation**: After every job, send WhatsApp with direct Google review link. Incentivize: "Leave a review, get ₹50 off next clean"
- **Target**: 50+ reviews with 4.8+ rating within 6 months
- **Track**: Use Google Business Insights to see how many calls/direction requests you get

**Target Partners (Faridabad + Delhi NCR)**:

| Installer Type | How to Find | Partnership Approach |
|---------------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Loom Solar** (Faridabad HQ) | Direct visit or LinkedIn | Pitch to their service/AMC head. They don't want to do cleaning themselves. |

| **Individual Solar Consultants/Agents** | LinkedIn, solar industry WhatsApp groups | 10% commission on every referral |
| **Electrical Shops selling inverters** | Local market (NIT, Ballabgarh) | Customers buying inverters often have or plan solar |

**Partnership Kit (Deliver to Each Partner)**:
1. **Co-branded Maintenance Reminder Card**: Given by installer to solar customer at time of installation. "Your panels need cleaning every 3 months. Recommended partner: SuryaClean"
2. **Referral Pad**: Unique referral code cards for each installer to track commissions
3. **1-page flyer**: Why cleaning matters + SuryaClean pricing + contact
4. **Commission Structure Sheet**: 10% on first clean, 5% lifetime on AMC
5. **Business Card Bundle**: 50 of your cards for them to hand out

**Commission Tracking**:
- Each partner gets a unique referral code (e.g., LOOM-001, TATA-002)
- Customer enters code when booking on app/website
- Commission auto-calculated in admin dashboard
- Monthly payout via bank transfer + WhatsApp statement

**Target**: Onboard 10-15 installer partners by Month 3, 25+ by Month 6

### Channel 4: WhatsApp Marketing

**List Building Strategies**:
1. **Google Business calls**: Save every inquiry number, ask permission to WhatsApp
2. **Website "Get Quote" form**: Collect phone number, auto-add to WhatsApp broadcast
3. **Society RWA WhatsApp groups**: Join (where allowed), contribute value, don't spam
4. **Solar Enthusiast Groups**: Facebook groups about solar in India → share informative content → DM interested people
5. **Existing customer referrals**: "Share our contact with 3 friends, get ₹100 off"
6. **Justdial/Sulekha leads**: Export inquiries to phone contacts

**WhatsApp Communication Calendar**:
| Timing | Message Type | Content |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| Day 1 (from inquiry) | Welcome | Brand intro + pricing + free demo offer |
| Day 3 | Educational | "3 Signs Your Solar Panels Need Cleaning" + photo |
| Day 7 | Social Proof | Customer testimonial video or before/after photo |
| Day 10 | Urgency | "Free demo offer ending this month. 12 slots left." |
| Day 14 | Final Follow-up | "Last message — still interested?" |
| Post-Service | Thank you + Review | Before/after photos, Google review link, AMC pitch |
| Quarterly (existing) | Reminder | "Your panels are due for cleaning next week. Book now?" |
| Festival | Greeting + Offer | Diwali/New Year discount |

**WhatsApp Business API (When Scaling)**:
- Use **WATI** or **Interakt** (₹2,499-₹4,999/month) for:
  - Automated WhatsApp sequences
  - Chatbot for common queries
  - Broadcast to 1,000+ contacts without spam blocks
  - CRM integration

**Compliance**:
- Always get opt-in ("Can we send you updates on WhatsApp?")
- Provide easy opt-out ("Reply STOP to unsubscribe")
- Don't message before 9 AM or after 8 PM
- No more than 2 messages/week to non-customers

## 6. Customer Retention & Reactivation

### Retention Strategies
- **AMC Auto-Renewal**: Set-and-forget. Customer approves one-time eMandate.
- **Birthday/Anniversary Offers**: "Happy Birthday! ₹200 off your next cleaning."
- **Seasonal Reminders**: App notification 2 weeks before next AMC visit due
- **Loyalty Program**: After 5 cleans, 6th is free
- **Annual Solar Report**: For AMC customers — sent every year summarizing generation data, cleaning history, savings achieved

### Reactivation Campaigns
- **Churned Customers** (no booking in 6+ months):
  - WhatsApp: "Aapke panels ko 8 mahine ho gaye safai kiye hue. Ek baar check karein — generation kam toh nahi ho gaya?"
  - Offer: "Welcome back — 25% off your next clean"
- **Incomplete Bookings** (started booking but didn't pay):
  - SMS/WhatsApp within 1 hour: "Aapki booking incomplete hai. Kya main help kar sakta hoon?"
