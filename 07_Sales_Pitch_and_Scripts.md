## 3. Customer Segment — Specific Pitches

### A. Residential Homeowner (Door-to-Door / Cold Calling)

**Opening**:
> "Namaste Sir/Ma'am! Aapke ghar ki chhat par solar panels lage hue hain? Bahut accha! Main SuryaClean se hoon — hum professional solar panel cleaning service hain. Kya aap jaante hain ki dhool ki wajah se aapke panels ki efficiency 15-30% tak gir jaati hai?"

*(Hello Sir/Ma'am! Do you have solar panels on your roof? Great! I'm from SuryaClean — we're a professional solar panel cleaning service. Did you know that dust can reduce your panel efficiency by 15-30%?)*

**Pain Point Questions**:
1. "Aapne last cleaning kab karwayi thi?" *(When did you last get them cleaned?)*
2. "Kya aapne kabhi check kiya ki aapki solar unit kitni bijli generate kar rahi hai?" *(Have you ever checked how much electricity your unit is generating?)*
3. "Kya aap khud chhat par jaake saaf karte hain?" *(Do you clean them yourself by going on the roof?)*
4. "Kya aapko pata hai ki gandi panels ki wajah se aapke bijli bill mein farak padta hai?" *(Did you know dirty panels affect your electricity bill?)*

**Objection Handlers**:

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Bahut mehnga hai / Too expensive" | "Sir, ₹899 ka ek clean, aur gandi panels se har mahine ₹1,000+ ka nuksan. Ek saal mein ₹12,000 bach sakte hain. Aur pehla clean toh free hai!" |
| "Mera mali/cleaner kar deta hai" | "Mali aam safai kar sakta hai, lekin solar panels ke liye special technique, soft brush, aur purified water chahiye — nahi toh micro-scratches se panel kharab ho sakta hai. Hum professional hain, insured hain." |
| "Abhi zaroorat nahi hai" | "Kya main ek baar free inspection kar sakta hoon? Pata chalega kitni efficiency loss ho rahi hai. 5 minute ka kaam hai." |
| "Pehle se theek chal raha hai" | "Bilkul — lekin jaise gaadi ki servicing time pe karte hain, waise hi solar panels ki bhi regular cleaning chahiye. Ek saal mein bina cleaning ke ₹8,000-₹12,000 ka nuksan ho sakta hai 5 kW system pe." |
| "Barish mein saaf ho jate hain" | "Barish sirf dhool hatati hai, lekin bird droppings, oily grime, aur chemical deposits nahi hat-te. Aur monsoon ke baad sabse zyada gandagi hoti hai. Humara pre-monsoon + post-monsoon AMC plan perfect hai." |

**Closing**:

## 5. In-Person Canvassing Strategy

### Door-to-Door Kit (Each Worker/Sales Person Carries)
1. **Branded ID Card** (visible, lanyard)
2. **Tablet/Smartphone** with demo app or photo gallery
3. **Before/After Photo Album** (physical, laminated — works without internet)
4. **Flyer** — 1 page, bilingual, with pricing and QR code
5. **Business Card**
6. **Small Gift**: Fridge magnet with SuryaClean branding, cleaning schedule, phone number
7. **Portable Soiling Tester** (optional): Show homeowner real-time dust density

### Canvassing Route Planning
- **Target**: Colonies with visible solar panels on rooftops (spotted from street)
- **Best Time**: Saturday 9 AM - 12 PM, Sunday 9 AM - 12 PM (people at home)
- **Avoid**: Monday mornings, weekday afternoons (empty homes), rainy days
- **Ideal Areas (Faridabad)**:
  - Greenfields Colony
  - Charmwood Village
  - Sector 14, 15, 16, 21C
  - Greater Faridabad (Sector 78-89)
  - Ashoka Enclave
  - Surajkund Road colonies

### Canvassing SOP
1. Spot solar panels from street
2. Ring doorbell (or security gate)
3. Use residential pitch (Section 3A above)
4. If no one home: Leave flyer + magnet in mailbox/gate
5. Note house number + location in CRM (Google Maps pin)
6. Follow up same area next weekend
7. Track: Houses visited, doors answered, appointments booked, conversions

## 7. Common Objections Master List

| # | Objection | Category | Best Response |
|---|-----------|----------|---------------|
| 1 | "Too expensive" | Price | ROI calculation — cleaning is ₹899, monthly loss from dirty panels is ₹1,000+. You're losing more by NOT cleaning. |
| 2 | "My maid/mali does it" | Competition | They may clean, but do they use soft brushes, purified water? Hard water and rough cloths cause micro-scratches that permanently damage panels. We're insured — they're not. |
| 3 | "Rain cleans them" | Misconception | Rain removes loose dust, but not bird droppings, oily grime, industrial deposits, or hard water stains. In fact, after rain, panels are often dirtier because dust turns to mud. |
| 4 | "No time" (for booking) | Convenience | You don't need to be home. Just give gate access — our app tracks everything. You'll get before/after photos on WhatsApp. 45 minutes, hassle-free. |
| 5 | "I'll do it myself" | DIY Risk | Understood, Sir. But rooftop work is dangerous — one slip can be devastating. Our workers wear full-body harnesses and are insured. Also, our thermal imaging catches issues you can't see with naked eyes. |
| 6 | "Already have AMC with installer" | Competition | Does their AMC include cleaning? Most installer AMCs are for electrical/inverter maintenance only. Check your contract — cleaning is usually extra or excluded. |
| 7 | "Don't believe it makes a difference" | Skepticism | Let's do a test: I'll clean HALF your panels for free. Check your generation before and after. If the cleaned half doesn't show improvement, you owe nothing. |
| 8 | "Will think about it" (stall) | Indecision | Bilkul, sochiye. Main aapko ek week mein call karta hoon. Meanwhile, yeh free cleaning ka offer sirf [month-end] tak hai. Aur agar aap abhi book karte hain toh main ₹100 ka referral discount bhi de sakta hoon. |
| 9 | "Don't trust outsiders on my roof" | Trust | Our workers are police-verified, Aadhaar-verified, and every visit is GPS-tracked with mandatory before/after photos. We're a registered company with insurance. Here's our office address — aap kabhi bhi aa sakte hain. |
| 10 | "Just had them installed, they're clean" | Timing | New panels attract the most dust — static electricity from manufacturing. Also, construction dust from nearby buildings. Month 1 is actually when they need cleaning the most to ensure maximum payback from day one. |

## 10. Conversion Funnel Metrics

| Stage | Target Conversion Rate | Action |
|-------|----------------------|--------|
| Ad Impression → Website Visit | 3-5% CTR | Optimized ad copy + targeting |
| Website Visit → Booking Enquiry | 8-12% | Strong CTA, instant pricing, trust badges |
| Enquiry → Free Demo Booked | 40-50% | Phone follow-up within 2 hours |
| Free Demo → Paid Service | 60-70% | Quality demo + "limited offer" urgency |
| First Paid → AMC Signup | 30-40% | Post-service WhatsApp sequence + discount |
| AMC → Referral Generated | 50%+ | In-app referral program + WhatsApp prompt |

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Target**: <₹300
- If ad spend ₹10,000/month generates 50 demo bookings
- 60% conversion to paid = 30 customers
- CAC = ₹10,000 / 30 = ₹333
- With organic (free) leads, blended CAC should be <₹200
